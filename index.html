About Us

Who We Are

WI Education Global was founded in 2015 with a vision to connect educational opportunities across borders. We're a team of dedicated professionals with extensive experience in international education, cultural exchange programs, and academic consulting.

Our mission is to empower students and institutions through accessible, quality education that transcends geographical boundaries. We believe that education is the most powerful tool for positive change, and we're committed to making it accessible to all.

Our Services

Test Preparation

Expert coaching for standardized tests like IELTS, TOEFL, SAT, GRE, and GMAT with personalized study plans and practice materials to maximize your scores.

Interview Assistance

One-on-one mock interviews and coaching sessions to help you confidently navigate university and visa interviews with strategic guidance on common questions.

Scholarship and Financial Aid

Comprehensive support in identifying and applying for scholarships, grants, and financial aid opportunities to make your education abroad more affordable.

Documentation Guidance

Step-by-step assistance with preparing and organizing all required documents for applications, visas, and immigration processes with attention to detail.

College Admissions Consulting

Personalized guidance throughout the entire college selection and admissions process, tailored to your academic profile, career goals, and preferences.

University Application Assistance

End-to-end support with university applications, including profile evaluation, essay review, and submission guidance to increase your chances of acceptance.
